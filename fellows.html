CUNY AI Lab

Fellow Program

The CUNY AI Lab fellow program supports the lab's research, pedagogy, communications, and development work across the CUNY system. Fellows hold distinct roles organized around the lab's core activities: community-building, teaching with AI, and building tools and infrastructure for open use.

📋

Academic Assistant

Administration & Outreach

The Academic Assistant supports the administrative backbone of the lab — keeping operations running, correspondence flowing, and institutional relationships maintained.

  • Administrative operations and lab coordination
  • Email correspondence and institutional outreach
  • Scheduling, recordkeeping, and internal communications
📡

Communications Fellow

Public Presence & Community

The Communications Fellow manages the lab's public presence and community relationships — surfacing work, building audiences, and connecting the lab to campuses and partners across CUNY.

  • Manage lab social media across Bluesky, Instagram, and LinkedIn
  • Organize events, workshops, and public programming
  • Hold weekly office hours for lab community and campus visitors
  • Conduct outreach to CUNY campuses and partner institutions
  • Surface and amplify student and faculty work hosted on Kale Deploy
  • Draft and release public-facing materials — new tool announcements, lab updates, special releases, and event documentation
🎓

AI Pedagogy Fellow

Teaching, Learning & Faculty Development

The AI Pedagogy Fellow works at the intersection of teaching and technology — supporting instructors and students working with AI tools, and helping the CUNY community develop critical, practical AI literacy.

  • Support users working in the Open WebUI sandbox environment
  • Help facilitate CAIL sandbox courses and curricular integrations
  • Co-facilitate teacher training workshops and professional development sessions
  • Develop open educational resources for AI literacy and pedagogy
  • Handle consultations with students, and particularly with instructors integrating AI into their courses
  • Participate in faculty professional development programming across CUNY
⚙️

Development Fellow

Research, Infrastructure & Technical Development

The Development Fellow supports the technical research and infrastructure of the lab — from curating training data and evaluating models to contributing to the tools and systems the lab builds for the broader CUNY community.

  • Curate training data and generate synthetic datasets for model fine-tuning
  • Contribute to the CUNY AI Lab GitHub — tooling, documentation, and code review
  • Establish and maintain AI benchmarking practices for evaluating model performance
  • Update the model registry and evaluate models for adoption into the sandbox environment
  • Support technical infrastructure, research, and development in coordination with the special project team
